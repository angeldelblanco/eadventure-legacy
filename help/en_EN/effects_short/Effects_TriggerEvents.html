Trigger events

There are also effects which allow us to trigger certain events.

The effects on this group are the following:

  • Trigger conversation: through this effect a conversation is triggered.


  • Trigger cutscene: through this effect a cutscene is triggered, showing information like slides or video.


  • Trigger book: a information on a book is showed.


  • Trigger scene: through this information a change of scene will be performed.


  • Trigger last scene: through this effect we go into the final scene.
