Effect trigger scene

Throudh this effect we switch to the selected scene.

Create a trigger scene effect:

  • Select the effect: Select the effect on the creation effects panel. Press OK.


  • Creation effect panel: afterwards an edition panel will appear:


    In the first place we can choose the scene we will switch once the effect is performed.
    Afterwards will appear a preview of the scene and from the position in which the player will appear after the switch has been performed. This position can be chosen giving value to the coordinates X and Y, or just by clicking in the position of the desired scene.
