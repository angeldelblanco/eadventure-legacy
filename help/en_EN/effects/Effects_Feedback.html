Notification effects

The notification effects allow giving information to user on some several ways. Those kind of effects are used to give a feedback notion of the game to user.

The effects within this group are the following:

  • Player says: The main character will say the text introduced in this effect.
  • Character: the character says the text introduced in this effect.
  • Trigger book: the information of the book is showed.
  • Show Text: the text is showed in the chosen position, and with the selected colors combination. We have to highlight this text is no reproduced by any player, it could be consider an off-voice.
  • Trigger cutscene: using a cutscene we provide information to the user. These scenes can be video or slides.
  • Trigger a conversation: we provide information to the user through a conversation.
