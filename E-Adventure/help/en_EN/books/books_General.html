Books

Books are an useful tool we can use in games to show information to the player in a quick way. It exists two types:

  • Simple content: If this option is chosen, the book is build via attaching paragraphs with plain text or images. Other facilities supported are simple bullets and titles (text with a larger font size)
  • Formatted text: This type of book suports richer content: formatted text in RTF or HTML format. The disadvantage is the content files must be authored with an external tool.
