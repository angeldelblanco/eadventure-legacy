Effect trigger cutscene

 

Through this effect a cutscene is triggered.

Create a trigger cutscene effect:

  • Select the effect: Select the effect on the creation effects panel. Press OK.


  • Creation effect panel: afterwards the next panel will appear:






  • We can choose among the cutscenes of the chapter.Se puede elegir entre las escenas intermedias que existan para este capítulo.
    It is noticeable that once the cutscene the effects and the switches of the scene associated are executed. (!!!!!!enlace a la parte en la que se explique esto???????) Once the associated cutscene effects are over they will continue executing the other effects in order. Una vez terminados los efectos asociados a la escena intermedia se continuarán ejecutando los efectos que siguen en orden al efecto lanzar escena intermedia en cuestion. That is, although the fact of launching an object triggers other indirect effects, this does not involve cutting the flow of the effects expected to be launched.

    Example:
    We have the following effects asociated to watch an object::
    1. Launch cutscene "XXXX", which has the following associated effect:
      1. Player says: "First commentar"
    2. Player says: "Second commentar"
    Effects will execute in this order:: 1º Launch cutscene; 2ºPlayer says: "First commentar"; 3º Player says: "Second commentar"
